Our solutions

Modular lending technology for the full credit lifecycle

Mida offers a unified system built to prevent bad debt, manage risk proactively, and resolve delinquency ethically:

Onboarding & Credit Decisioning

Configurable onboarding, decisioning and loan management for lenders. Deliver plug and play channels across mobile apps, web apps and third-party platforms.

Portfolio Management & Lending Operations

Enterprise debt management solution with rule engine, collections CRM, dynamic bucket creation, and powerful portfolio information features.

Learn More

Digital Collections

Digital and instant loan tokenization leveraging direct debits, card-less collection, automated user engagement sequencing, and real-time analytics.

Digital Skip Tracing

Skip tracing tracking solution to help lenders locate hard-to-find defaulters, increasing chances and success rates of debt recovery teams.

Recovery & NPL Management
