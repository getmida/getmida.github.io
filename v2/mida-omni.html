MidaOmni

MidaOmni is the operational and intelligence layer that helps lenders manage portfolios, strategies, teams, and performance from one place.

Talk to Us

Trusted by leading institutions in Africa

The Problem It Solves

Most lenders struggle with fragmented portfolio visibility, siloed operational tools, and reactive collections processes. Loan data lives across multiple systems, strategies are hard-coded or manually executed, and teams lack real-time insight into portfolio health. The result is slow decision-making, poor coordination across teams, rising delinquency, and missed opportunities to act early.

MidaOmni centralizes portfolio intelligence and operations into a single system. It aggregates loan data in real time, enables dynamic segmentation and strategy execution, and gives lenders clear visibility into performance across portfolios, teams, and partners turning reactive portfolio management into proactive, automated control.

Key Capabilities

Portfolio configuration

Centralized control over loan data, segments, and portfolio structure

Strategy and rule management

Define automated workflows and business rules across your portfolio

Collections CRM

Manage customer interactions and collections workflows in one system

Agent and partner oversight

Track and coordinate internal teams and external partners

Performance analytics and reporting

Real-time insights into portfolio health and operational efficiency

Built for operations, risk, and credit leaders who need clarity and control at scale. Manage delinquency, coordinate teams, and optimize recovery from one unified command center.

How It Fits


MidaOmni is the operational core of the Mida platform. All disbursed loans flow into MidaOmni from the lender’s core data source, where they are continuously monitored, segmented, and acted upon in real time.


Strategies defined in MidaOmni drive collections workflows, agent actions, partner assignments, and performance tracking across the portfolio. Every repayment, interaction, and outcome feeds back into the system, ensuring lenders maintain full visibility, accountability, and control throughout the loan lifecycle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can’t find the answer here? Speak with Us

1. How does Mida’s Strategy Management module work?
Mida's intuitive rule engine allows you to segment your credit portfolio easily into pre-defined buckets and assign tailored actions to each segment based on various criteria like risk, delinquency, customer behavior, location, gender, etc.
2. Can Mida manage both internal teams and external debt recovery agents?
Yes, Mida's platform enables you to manage your collections contact center, field recovery agents, and external DCAs from a single hub, allowing for seamless tracking, comparison, and strategy adjustments.
3. What kind of real-time analytics does Mida provide?
Mida provides advanced real-time dashboards and analytics, offering insights into your debt portfolio’s health, agent performance, and overall recovery efficiency to help you make informed decisions.
4. How does Mida compare to other international solutions?
Mida offers advanced segmentation, agent management, and comprehensive analytics like similar global enterprise solutions. However, Mida distinguishes itself through an even more user-friendly rule engine and a unified, easily scalable platform.
5. How customizable are Mida’s solutions for different business needs?
Mida’s platforms are highly customizable, allowing businesses to tailor collections, recovery, and loan management strategies according to their specific portfolio, team structure, and operational goals.
6. Is Mida’s enterprise solution scalable for large portfolios?
Absolutely. Mida is designed to scale with your needs, from small teams to large enterprise-level operations, ensuring your collections and recovery strategies are adaptable as your portfolio grows.
