Frequently Asked Questions

Can’t find the answer here? Speak with Us

1. How does Mida determine the value of an overdue loan portfolio?
Mida uses a debt scoring process to assess the value of the loan assets, considering factors such as risk, borrower history, and the number of days past due. This ensures a fair and accurate valuation.
2. Who purchases the overdue loan portfolios?
After determining the value of your portfolio, Mida partners with independent financiers to facilitate the purchase of your overdue loans, ensuring you receive immediate value for your assets.
3. What types of loans does Mida focus on for purchase?
Mida primarily works with lenders who offer uncollateralized loan products. We are open to purchasing loans regardless of how many days past due the accounts are.
4. How does Mida ensure ethical collections after purchasing a portfolio?
Mida is committed to ethical and data-driven collections practices. We focus on protecting borrower rights while collecting on the purchased loans.
5. Can I request a meeting to discuss this?
Yes, you can by clicking filling out the form in our ‘Contact Us’ page
