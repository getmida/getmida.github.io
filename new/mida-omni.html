Frequently Asked Questions

Can’t find the answer here? Speak with Us

1. How does Mida’s Strategy Management module work?
Mida's intuitive rule engine allows you to segment your credit portfolio easily into pre-defined buckets and assign tailored actions to each segment based on various criteria like risk, delinquency, customer behavior, etc.
2. Can Mida manage both internal teams and external debt recovery agents?
Yes, Mida's platform enables you to manage your collections contact center, field recovery agents, and external DCAs from a single hub, allowing for seamless tracking, comparison, and strategy adjustments.
3. What kind of real-time analytics does Mida provide?
Mida provides advanced real-time dashboards and analytics, offering insights into your debt portfolio’s health, agent performance, and overall recovery efficiency to help you make informed decisions.
4. How does Mida compare to other international solutions?
Mida offers advanced segmentation, agent management, and comprehensive analytics like similar global enterprise solutions. However, Mida distinguishes itself through an even more user-friendly rule engine and a unified, easily scalable platform.
5. How customizable are Mida’s solutions for different business needs?
Mida’s platforms are highly customizable, allowing businesses to tailor collections, recovery, and loan management strategies according to their specific portfolio, team structure, and operational goals.
6. Is Mida’s enterprise solution scalable for large portfolios?
Absolutely. Mida is designed to scale with your needs, from small teams to large enterprise-level operations, ensuring your collections and recovery strategies are adaptable as your portfolio grows.
7. Can I see a demo of the enterprise solution before deciding?
Yes, you can request a live demo by clicking on the ‘Request a demo’ button or explore Mida’s platform yourself with our self-service option by clicking on ‘Get started.’
