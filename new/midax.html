Frequently Asked Questions

Can’t find the answer here? Speak with Us

1. How does MidaX help field recovery agents locate hard-to-find borrowers?
MidaX (x marks the spot!) leverages our cutting-edge, proprietary skip tracing technology to help field recovery agents track down borrowers who have moved or whose contact details are no longer valid. It automates the search process by quickly identifying a borrower’s footprint, eliminating the need for manual investigations, and significantly reducing the time it takes to locate them.
2. Can MidaX help lower recovery costs?
Yes, MidaX minimizes cash expenditures by streamlining the location process, reducing the logistics costs (car hire, fuel, airtime, security, etc) typically involved in physically searching for customers - sometimes unsuccessfully - during field recovery efforts.
3. Does using MidaX improve the chances of collecting outstanding debts?
Absolutely - the chances of collecting on an overdue loan increases by up to 55% once a lender can locate a defaulter face-to-face. By helping field recovery teams find customers in their current locations, MidaX dramatically increases the likelihood of successful debt collection.
4. Is MidaX suitable for large-scale field recovery operations?
Yes, MidaX is designed to scale efficiently, making it ideal for both small and large field recovery teams that need to locate borrowers across multiple locations.
5. Where is MidaX available today?
MidaX is only available in Nigeria currently however, it will become available across Africa subsequently
6. Can I see a demonstration of MidaX before using it?
Yes, you can request a demo by clicking on the ‘Request a demo’ button, or you can explore MidaX independently through our self-service option by clicking on ‘Get started.’
