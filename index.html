Why choose Mida?

Our technology

We rapidly test multiple collections tactics in real time and intensify actions based on those that successfully influence successful collections & recovery in different user personas. We then automate these tactics at scale ensuring we can serve more people faster.

Our data analytics

Our algorithm leverage a large behavioral data set, processing over 300 variables of customer data, 3rd party data, digital footprint data and behaviorial data to identify trends & patterns that influence how and when we engage pre and post default.

Our localized experience

With over 40 years of experience in African markets, we combine our technology with a hands-on approach leveraging our field teams to establish face to face contact with users when needed, addressing low contact rate challenges with skip tracing & in-depth community integration.
