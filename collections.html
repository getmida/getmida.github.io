Frequently Asked Questions

Can’t find the answer here? Speak with Us

1. What types of repayment instruments can Mida set up for my loan customers?
Mida can set up repayment instruments such as cards, direct debit, or GSI using either your credentials or ours, tailored to your operational needs.
2. How does Mida improve collections success rates of these instruments?
Mida’s advanced collections intelligence and algorithms optimize the repayment process by layering additional logic on the generic instruments available in the market. This means our lenders can immediately enjoy better and more successful collection success rates.
3. What is Mida’s approach to user engagement during collections?
Mida generates personas for every user and based on their profiles, dynamically engages customers through multiple touchpoints like calls, WhatsApp, emails, visits, etc. with the right message at the right time through their preferred channels, helping increase their likelihood to repay on or before the due date.
4. Can I explore Mida’s collections features before committing?
Yes, you can request a demo from our team by clicking on the ‘Request a demo’ button or explore Mida’s features yourself using the self-service option by clicking on ‘Get started.’
5. How does Mida ensure the security of customer payment information during the collections process?
Mida follows strict security protocols to protect customer data, ensuring all repayment instruments and sensitive information are handled securely and compliantly.
6. What industries or sectors does Mida primarily serve?
Mida's solutions are versatile and cater to a wide range of industries, including financial services, fintech, microfinance, telecommunications, and any other sector that deals with collections and recovery operations
