Frequently Asked Questions

Can’t find the answer here? Speak with Us

1. How does Mida improve recovery rates for NPLs and written-off loans?
Mida combines digital collections with a data-driven contact center and nationwide field recovery agents to locate customers and recover outstanding repayments faster and more effectively.
2. What makes Mida’s contact center ‘Ultra-modern’?
Mida’s contact center leverages a very experienced multilingual team working with a rich network of data and insights, that allows it to manage calls efficiently across the nation and provide a personalized experience to improve recovery success.
3. How does the MidaX help in recovery efforts?
 MidaX is an advanced digital skip tracing tool that our professional field agents use to locate customers who have moved away from their last known address, enhancing our ability to recover outstanding debts.
4. What is Mida’s approach to ethical collections and recovery?
Mida places a strong emphasis on ethical collections, ensuring that all recovery activities are conducted with the utmost respect for customers and protecting your reputation throughout the process.
5. Does Mida offer recovery services nationwide?
Yes, Mida operates nationwide (Nigeria) with a team of skilled field agents who work alongside our digital collections and contact center to recover loans across the country.
6. Can I explore Mida’s recovery solutions before making a commitment?
Absolutely! You can request a demo from our team by clicking on the ‘Request a demo’ button or explore Mida’s recovery solutions on your own through our self-service option by clicking on ‘Get started.’
